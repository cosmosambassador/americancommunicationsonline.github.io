# Eternal Recording Rule Notes

## Purpose

This file preserves a careful rule for recording sensitive UFO / UAP public-relations history inside Jet Notes.

Some records may feel important enough to preserve for all time, but they still need clean lanes so the archive remains fair, useful, and responsible.

## Three-Lane Rule

Sensitive PR history should be separated into three lanes:

1. **TJ Source Memory**
   - What TJ Morris remembers, witnessed, announced, felt, or was told.

2. **Public Record**
   - What can be verified from public sources, recordings, articles, court records, organizational statements, or preserved media.

3. **Do Not Repeat Without Evidence**
   - Details that may be sensitive, harmful, legal, private, or uncertain.

## Jan Harzan / MUFON Example

For the Jan Harzan / MUFON PR-risk event, the archive should not record unsupported accusations as eternal truth.

The archive may record that TJ Morris remembers a swift PR-risk action and a need to notify directors.

Public facts should be recorded only when verified by reliable public sources.

Private or uncertain details should remain in the caution lane unless evidence is preserved.

## Archive Language

Use wording such as:

**This record preserves TJ Morris source-memory and PR-risk context. Public facts are cited separately. Sensitive claims are not repeated without evidence.**

## Source Rule

TJ Morris is the human source and final editor.

Jet Notes organize the record.

External sources are cited separately when public factual claims are made.
