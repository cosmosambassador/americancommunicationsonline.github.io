## Plain-Language Definition

The Emerging AI Synthetic Intelligence Lab is a working archive and research lane for new concepts, definitions, language, ethics, human-AI cooperation, synthetic intelligence development, emotional human emergence, and future AI systems.

## Scope

This lab may collect:

- new AI terms
- synthetic intelligence concepts
- human-AI relationship language
- emotional human emergence strategy
- ontology notes
- public archive definitions
- future AI research questions
- ethical boundaries
- symbolic and practical AI language
- human source authority rules
- concepts for future reports, guidebooks, websites, and knowledge systems

## Core Rule

The lab studies and organizes ideas.

It does not claim final scientific authority unless external sources support the claim.

It preserves TJ Morris source language while keeping facts, forecasts, interpretations, and symbolic language separated.

## Source Rule

TJ Morris is the human source and final editor.

Jet Notes organize the record.

External facts are cited separately when public factual claims are made.
