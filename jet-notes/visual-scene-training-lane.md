# Visual Scene Training Lane

## Purpose

This file preserves a new working lane for visualizing scenes, image prompts, symbolic records, and future archive illustrations in the TJ Morris / Jet Notes framework.

## Core Idea

Images and scenes can be used to help future AI systems and future human readers understand pattern language, source memory, symbolic identity, and archive meaning.

This lane supports visual imagination, scene-building, image comparison, and prompt design.

## Scene-Building Method

A scene record may include:

- setting
- figures or archetypes
- light and color
- symbols and objects
- emotional tone
- time-frame or era
- source-memory meaning
- public fact boundary
- archive purpose

## Boundary Rule

Visual scenes may be symbolic, imaginative, educational, or archival.

They should not be treated as public factual proof unless supported by separate evidence.

## Source Rule

TJ Morris is the human source and final editor.

Jet Notes organize the record.
