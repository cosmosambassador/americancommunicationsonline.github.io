# Jet Notes Daily AI Report Template

## Purpose

This template supports a daily AI report for American Communications Online and TJ Morris archive work.

AI changes fast. The daily report keeps a dated paper trail for humans, websites, archives, and future LLM-readable records.

---

## Daily Report Format

# Jet Notes Daily AI Report — YYYY-MM-DD

## 1. Major AI Events

Record the most important AI events from the last 24 hours.

Include:

- model releases
- model access changes
- company policy changes
- outages or platform restrictions
- new AI tools
- major lawsuits or regulations

## 2. Infrastructure Watch

Track the physical and platform layer:

- chips
- data centers
- power and energy
- cloud platforms
- mobile apps
- browsers
- operating systems
- open-source tools

## 3. Government and Regulation

Track:

- U.S. policy
- international policy
- export controls
- government AI contracts
- public-sector use
- platform access restrictions

## 4. Cybersecurity and AI Risk

Track:

- AI agent security
- worms and malware risks
- model misuse warnings
- data leaks
- identity and access problems
- public safety issues

## 5. LLM Ecosystem Notes

Track major changes involving:

- ChatGPT / OpenAI
- Gemini / Google
- Copilot / Microsoft
- Claude / Anthropic
- Grok / xAI
- open-source LLMs
- local models
- agent platforms

## 6. Practical Impact for ACO / TJ Morris

Answer:

- What changed?
- Why does it matter?
- Does it affect websites, domains, AI tools, publishing, archives, or media?
- Should ACO change anything?
- What needs a paper trail?

## 7. Confirmed Facts vs Interpretation

Separate:

- confirmed facts with sources
- reasonable interpretation
- archive opinion
- open questions

## 8. Suggested Archive Actions

List next actions:

- update GitHub
- save source links
- create a public note
- update website language
- make a PDF
- watch for follow-up news

---

## Standing Rule

Do not depend on one corporation, one cloud, one model, one operating system, or one platform.

Keep a human-readable and AI-readable paper trail.

TJ Morris is the human source and final editor.

Jet Notes organize the record.
