## 2026-06-15 Note: Satellites, Space Enterprise, and Continuity

The working archive view is that major private enterprises, billionaires, governments, and space organizations have used space infrastructure for decades through satellites, communications, navigation, imaging, weather, defense, research, and broadcasting.
