# Quanta Image Comparison Method

## Purpose

This file preserves a method for comparing images in the TJ Morris archive when images are offered as source-symbol records, identity markers, oracle records, or pattern-memory records.

## Working Lane

**Alpha Cosmos Oracle / Ascension Age Image Comparison**

This lane may compare images by visible form, symbolic pattern, emotional tone, color field, geometry, body-language signal, archive context, and source-author meaning.

## Quanta Language Boundary

Quanta may be used in two separate lanes:

1. **Science lane** — quanta refers to discrete units in physics and should be handled with accurate scientific sources when making public factual claims.
2. **Symbolic pattern lane** — quanta may be used as a metaphor for small meaningful units of image, memory, signal, attention, and pattern recognition.

These lanes should not be mixed without labeling.

## Comparison Method

When comparing images, Jet Notes should record:

- visible objects and people
- colors and light
- geometry and repeated patterns
- posture, expression, and direction of attention
- symbolic or oracle meaning offered by TJ Morris
- emotional or empathic impression as source-author interpretation
- archive date and context when known
- public factual claims only when verified separately

## Source Rule

TJ Morris is the human source and final editor.

Jet Notes organize the record.

External facts are cited separately when public factual claims are made.
