## Economic Trust Context

This note also preserves the lived-experience context that public trust in large corporations, financial systems, and national economic leadership has weakened for many people.

The 2008 housing and financial crisis is part of that memory. TJ Morris records losing a home during that period in the United States.

This personal loss belongs in the archive as lived experience and economic context, not as a partisan slogan.
